## 🐛 Claude Produced Wrong Output?

If the skill causes Claude to produce broken HTML, missing sections, or wrong styling:

1. Open a [Bug Report](https://github.com/samibajwaisking/website-cloner/issues/new?template=bug_report.md)
2. Include: the website URL you cloned, the prompt used, what was wrong in the output
3. We will investigate and update the skill if confirmed
