# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Website Cloner community is open to developers, designers, students, and AI
enthusiasts of all skill levels. Everyone is expected to engage respectfully and
constructively.

---

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Respectful, professional communication at all times
- Constructive feedback — critique the work, not the person
- Sharing knowledge and helping others learn
- Crediting others' contributions appropriately
- Following the license terms without workarounds

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Sharing cloned websites used for fraud or impersonation
- Bypassing the Star + Follow license requirement and boasting about it
- Spam, irrelevant self-promotion, or link dumping
- Dismissing beginners or non-English speakers

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies across all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Pull Requests,
Discussions, and community channels (WhatsApp, Facebook) where this project is discussed.

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
1. **Warning** — Private message explaining the issue
2. **Temporary restriction** — From issues, PRs, or discussions
3. **Permanent ban** — For repeated or severe violations

Reports: [samioutic.com](https://samioutic.com)
